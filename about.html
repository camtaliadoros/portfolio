About Me

With 12 years of experience as a marketer, Camila has built her career in the media and entertainment industry with internationally renowned brands and artists. Having worked for both multinationals and startups with different backgrounds, Camila is a flexible professional who enjoys fast-paced environments and challenges. Her driven and brave personality led her to be entrusted with responsibilities from very early in her career. Camila has a proactive approach to work and is always looking to learn new things, which led her to build a varied skill set.

Camila is a creative and forward thinking individual with strong analytical and strategic skills, with a passion for delivering memorable experiences and highly interested in consumer behaviour. An ambitious professional with ability to manage multiple projects, Camila has a proven success in developing, implementing and managing marketing campaigns from initial concept through to completion.
